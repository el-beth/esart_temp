Details

D series evaporator (also known as air cooler) is a kind of cooling equipment suitable for all kinds of cold room (such as civil cold room or combined cold room). D series air coolers are available in DL, DD, and DJ, which are suitable for different storage temperature. It has compact structure, light weight, does not occupy the cold room area,the temperature is uniform, the food stored in the cold storage quickly cooling, greatly improving the freshness of stored food.
